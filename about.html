Who am I?

My name is KJ. I'm a college athlete, developer, creative designer, photographer, and videographer with a passion for storytelling through visuals that leave an impact. From dynamic sports graphics to cinematic highlight reels and lifestyle photography, I bring energy, precision, and intention to every project I take on. I've shot for ESPN, been trusted by Division I athletic programs, and used by brands and companies across the region — but my mission has always stayed the same: create powerful content that’s both high-quality and accessible. Whether you’re a rising athlete looking to showcase your game, a couple wanting to freeze a special moment, or a brand aiming to stand out — I offer creative services that combine professional-level production with down-to-earth pricing. I believe you shouldn't have to spend thousands to get visuals that represent you well, and I’m committed to working with people and teams at every level. Let’s make something bold, real, and unforgettable.

FAQ

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but most designs take between 2-4 weeks from concept to final delivery.

What file formats do you deliver?

I provide all standard formats including PNG, JPG, PDF, and vector files (AI/EPS) when applicable.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! Each project includes 2 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product.
